BTS SIO

General Overview

The BTS IT Services for Organizations (SIO) is a Bac+2 diploma training professionals to manage IT infrastructures or to participate in the production and delivery of IT services.

This program is for students interested in IT, application development, and network management. BTS SIO offers two complementary specialties.

SISR Option

Infrastructure, Systems, and Networks Solutions

Trains professionals in installation, maintenance, and security of IT equipment. Graduates can:

  • Install, integrate, administer, and secure IT equipment and services
  • Operate, troubleshoot, and monitor infrastructures
  • Manage incidents and assist users
  • Develop an organization’s web presence

Careers: Systems & Network Administrator, Maintenance Technician, IT Asset Manager, etc.

SLAM Option

Software Solutions and Business Applications

Trains software developers to:

  • Develop, adapt, and maintain applications
  • Handle and manipulate data
  • Design and implement application solutions
  • Ensure corrective and evolutionary maintenance

Careers: Application Developer, Analyst Programmer, Web Developer, etc.
